Learn to develop iOS applications under the guidance of an experienced Objective C developer in Berlin.

Stackademy iOS Development is made for developers with previous programming experience in other programming languages and will teach you all you need to plan and develop successful iOS applications for iPhone and iPad.

Our instructor has been developing in Objective C for 5+ years and was most recently the Lead iOS Developer for a Berlin-based company, where he oversaw development of the iOS app from the start.

As with all Stackademy courses, we include plenty of project work and focus on teaching what you need to start building, including the right use of the device hardware and working with tried-and-trusted third-party services that make you extra effective.

Become a builder

Stackademy takes you through 12 intense weeks of lessons and practice.
Your reward for pulling through? You will have learned how to plan, develop and deploy state-of-the-art web applications using Ruby and the Ruby on Rails framework, as well as a range of tools and processes designed to have you work effectively from day one.

Learn what it takes

We have compiled an intense curriculum of 12 weeks that not only teaches you the fundamentals but also the right use of tools and services that leading tech companies use every day.
To ensure that all participants are set for the course, you will work through prep material we supply once you are accepted to the course.

Step up your game

We have set up Stackademy to be the turbo-charged start on your way to becoming serious about coding.

To get you up and running fast and in the right direction, we are bringing in expert partners from Berlin and around the world for first-hand advice on starting your own company or becoming a successful startup employee or freelancer, as well as tons of tips and advice on how you integrate third-party services and APIs into your own projects. Last but not least, we are partnering with a number of selected companies for our recruiting day in the final week.

Guest lectures

Berlin is home to many great startups and their passionate founders. Throughout Stackademy, we'll have guest lectures on real-world challenges and examples.

Technology sessions

Great apps integrate great services and APIs. At Stackademy, you will learn about and integrate leading services like Paymill and MailChimp.

Recruiting Day

Stackademy gets you in touch with selected recruiting partners at the Recruiting Day in the final week: Talk to tech recruiters and see if you fit their profile. If you pass the hiring process and decide to join one of our partners, we will sweeten the deal and refund € 1,000 of your tuition.
